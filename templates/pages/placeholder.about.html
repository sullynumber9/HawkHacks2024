About Calendar Compare

Calendar compare started with a simple idea at HawkHacks 2024: what if we could make it easier to find a time when all of our friends are free? And thus, we came to this idea!

Once you sign in with Google and join a group, Calendar Compare will scan all of your events, compare them to the events of your friends, and see when you're all available!

We hope you enjoy using Calendar Compare as much as we enjoyed making it!
